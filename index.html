Hi There!

I am Nikoletta Kanellou, an Athens based designer and Cinnamon Design showcases the creative aspect of me.

My work varies in creative disciplines, complexity and scale, from user experience to architecture and brand identity. My user-centered design approach is all about creating a unique and dynamic experience for the user, regardless if this happens in a physical or digital space.

My academic background includes a Diploma in Architecture from the NTUA School of Architecture and an MA in Digital Arts from the Athens School of Fine Arts.
